    Elevators and Escalators

    We are using long term experiences gained either on Slovak market and also other counties worldwide in the field of installations, consulting, site supervising. We are focusing our activities to elevator industry (elevators, escalators and moving walks) and offer customers professional performance and solutions in line with all applicable safety and technical requirements.

    Company

    Where, with whom...

    Our experienced fitters are installing E&E units for 10 years primary in European countries like France, Belgium,Netherland, Switzerland... but also did works in UAE. Basically we install for all OEM and also local manufacturers.

    What...

    We specialize on High rise elevators but are well experienced also in modernizations (dismantling, installation, repairs). Certainly we do standard elevators,escalators and moving walks installation in both commercial and residential segments.

    How ...

    Long term experiences are our base for professionality and reliability. At the same time we put strong emphasis on the safety of our work and do periodical safety trainings for our staff, use appropriate personal protection equipment and conduct safety checks directly on site.
